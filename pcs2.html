WHAT IS PROFESSIONAL COMMUNICATION SKILLS 2 ?

Professional Communication Skills 2 sharpens the language and delivery skills needed in a professional setting — particularly business proposal writing, product pitching, and intonation training. The main deliverable was a full business proposal and pitch for a product (CU Mart) where we practised hook ad-libs, intonation, delivery, and Q&A handling. This course was about turning technical ideas into clear, persuasive communication for real audiences.

Reflection

PCS2 surprised me. I expected a light soft-skills course but got rigorous training in business proposal writing, pitching, and intonation. Building the CU Mart proposal and rehearsing the pitch was one of the most valuable experiences of the semester — communication is now something I deliberately practise, not assume.

Looking back, this is the course interviewers notice when I speak clearly about my work, even though they will never know I took it. Clear communication multiplies the value of every technical skill.
