WHAT IS BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE ?

Business Intelligence (BI) is the discipline of turning raw business data into insights that decision-makers can act on. The course covered BI architecture, data preprocessing, semantic layers, and dashboard design. The flagship deliverable was a three-phase BI project on real sales data — data ingestion, semantic modelling, and a final Power BI dashboard — backed up with labs, an industry-talk reflection report, and group discussion work.

BI Project

Three-phase BI project: data ingestion (customers, orders, products, order_items), modelling in a semantic layer, and a final Power BI dashboard (.pbix). Also includes an Alteryx workflow for the Sales & Products analysis.

Reflection

BI was one of my favourite courses. The three-phase project — data ingestion, semantic modelling, then a Power BI dashboard — felt exactly like the work data analysts and BI engineers do in industry. The Alteryx workflow taught me a different way to think about data preparation as a visual pipeline.

Looking back, this is the course that gave me the most directly job-applicable skills, and the Alteryx Designer Core certification I later earned came straight from this foundation. BI made me see data work as a craft, not just a task.
