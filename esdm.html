WHAT IS ENTERPRISE SYSTEMS DEVELOPMENT & MANAGEMENT ?

Enterprise Systems Development & Management is about how businesses design and manage the large-scale information systems that run their operations. The course covered enterprise architecture frameworks, current and future-state analysis, gap analysis, and how IT decisions support business strategy. The main highlight was an enterprise architecture project on a real company — Entellica — where we delivered both individual and combined group reports and a final EA presentation.
