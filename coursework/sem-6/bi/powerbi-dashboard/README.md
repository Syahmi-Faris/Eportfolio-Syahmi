**Subject:** Business Intelligence (SECP3123)
**Semester:** 6

## Overview

Three-phase BI project: data ingestion (customers, orders, products,
order_items CSVs), modelling, and a final Power BI dashboard.

## Skills

`Power BI` · `Alteryx` · `Data Modelling` · `BI Architecture` · `SQL`

- `BI Project Phase3.pdf` — final phase report
