WHAT IS DATABASE PROGRAMMING ?

Database Programming dives deep into advanced SQL operations — stored procedures, triggers, transactions, indexing, and query optimisation — on top of NoSQL technologies like MongoDB. In this course I worked with classical training datasets such as ClassicModel and Sakila to model real business problems, and built two full database projects, two group assignments, and weekly labs that strengthened my SQL fluency and database design skills.
