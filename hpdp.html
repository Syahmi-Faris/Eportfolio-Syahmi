WHAT IS HIGH PERFORMANCE DATA PROCESSING ?

High Performance Data Processing (HPDP) is the practical engineering of pipelines that collect, clean, and analyse data at scale. The course centred on two real end-to-end projects: a Playwright-based web-scraping pipeline that collected 100,000 car listings from Carlist and MotorTrader with pagination, state persistence, and resume-on-failure handling; and an NLP analysis of Grab reviews with preprocessing, feature extraction, and machine-learning models. Both projects were presented in class with full architecture explanations.
