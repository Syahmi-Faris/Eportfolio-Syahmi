WHAT IS DATABASE ?

The Database course introduced the foundations of relational databases — entity-relationship modelling, normalisation, SQL, and database design. The course centred on building a working database for a real-world scenario, including schema design and SQL implementation.

Database Project

Reflection

Database was where I first fell in love with data. Designing schemas, normalising tables, and writing SQL queries felt natural to me — and it is directly the foundation of everything I do now in data engineering. The project tied the whole course together by forcing me to design a real working database end-to-end.

Looking back, this was the course that made me realise I wanted to specialise in data. Every advanced data subject I took afterwards built on the foundations laid here.
